We are Central Virginia Javascript Meetup (CVJS). Javascript has good parts, bad parts, and parts you'll discover from the JS genius next door. We meet every 4th Wednesday of the month in Charlottesville, VA to share code, tools, techniques, and tall tales of Javascript wonders.

Code of Conduct

CVJS is dedicated to providing an inclusive and harassment-free experience for everyone regardless of gender, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, age, religion, or socioeconomic status. We do not tolerate harassment of participants in any form. Sexual language and imagery is not appropriate for any venue, including talks. Participants violating these rules may be sanctioned or expelled from the event at the discretion of the organizers.

We have joined other groups around Central Virginia to proactively create a shared Code of Conduct and Anti-Harassment Policy.
